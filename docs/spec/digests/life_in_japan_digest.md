# Life in Japan Context Digest

Default tier: balanced. Escalate to deep-reasoning for finance, lottery, housing, tax, legal, immigration, or personalized-risk flows.

## Mission

Turn real life in Japan into Japanese/BJT learning contexts without becoming advice, gambling promotion, or financial product UX.

## Must optimize

- practical Japanese comprehension
- BJT-style reading/listening scenarios
- risk literacy vocabulary
- document/form/email understanding
- remediation from mistakes
- source/provenance/date tracking
- learner control over sensitive topics

## Must reject

- buy/sell/hold investment advice
- lottery encouragement
- fake portfolio gains
- financial FOMO
- legal/tax/immigration claims without disclaimer
- affiliate/dark-pattern placements
- sensitive personal inference
- unproven current facts without source/date

## Review targets

- Daily Life Hub
- housing lessons
- tax/insurance/pension lessons
- banking/remittance lessons
- workplace document practice
- lottery/probability vocabulary
- stocks/crypto/news vocabulary
- share postcards for life-context learning
