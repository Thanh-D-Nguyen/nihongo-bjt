# BJT Growth Social Digest

Default tier: balanced. Escalate to deep-reasoning for competition mechanics and review-security for public sharing/privacy.

## Mission

Make sharing, referrals, battle, and leaderboards increase learning motivation without dark patterns or privacy leaks.

## Must optimize

- opt-in sharing
- privacy-safe public pages
- fair competition
- skill-aware motivation
- rematch and remediation loops
- respectful referrals
- learner control over public identity

## Must reject

- spammy referral prompts
- public shame
- fake ranks/opponents/share stats
- pay-to-win mechanics
- private data in URLs or OG metadata
- addictive loops that interrupt study
- leaderboard with no privacy mode

## Review targets

- share postcards
- public result pages
- referral campaigns
- battle UX
- leaderboards
- achievements/streaks
- social notifications

- sharing is opt-in
- competition is fair and learning-first
- public data is minimal and safe
- growth metrics are ethical
