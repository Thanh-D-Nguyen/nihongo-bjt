# Visual QA Rubric

Score every major UI change from 1–5.

## 5
Premium, consistent, responsive, accessible, production-ready.

## 4
Good, minor polish needed.

## 3
Functional but generic.

## 2
Inconsistent or incomplete states.

## 1
Broken, inaccessible, or demo-like.

Reject if:
- random emoji empty state
- mobile overflow
- missing loading/error states
- inconsistent spacing/buttons
- fake data presented as production
