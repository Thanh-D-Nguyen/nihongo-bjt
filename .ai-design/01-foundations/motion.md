# Motion

## Principles
Motion should help orientation, not entertain.

## Allowed
- 120–220ms transitions.
- subtle scale/opacity for cards and buttons.
- sheet slide-in on mobile.
- skeleton shimmer if already part of system.

## Avoid
- bouncing effects in serious learning flows.
- long animations.
- motion required to understand content.

## Accessibility
Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
