# Radius and Shadow

## Radius
- Small controls: 10–12px
- Cards: 16–20px
- Large panels/sheets: 24px
- Pills: 9999px

## Shadow
Use subtle shadows:
- default card: very soft
- floating panel: medium soft
- modal/sheet: stronger but not harsh

## Rules
- Avoid multiple nested shadows.
- Use border + background before heavy shadow.
- Elevated elements must have a reason: sticky bar, modal, popover, active card.
