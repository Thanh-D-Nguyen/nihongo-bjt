# North Star Metrics

## Goal

Measure real BJT learning progress, healthy product usage, content quality, and ethical growth.

## Learning outcome metrics

- Weekly active learners who complete meaningful study.
- Review completion rate.
- Retention after wrong-answer remediation.
- Accuracy improvement by skill.
- Estimated BJT band trend.
- Weakness recovery rate.
- Leech card reduction rate.
- Learning path milestone completion.

## Product health metrics

- D1/D7/D30 retention.
- Search success rate.
- Time to first useful learning action.
- First review completion.
- First quiz completion.
- Comeback success rate.
- Reading Assist use-to-card conversion.
- Study session completion without distraction exit.

## Content quality metrics

- Low-accuracy question rate.
- High-confusion distractor rate.
- Zero-result search queries.
- Zero-engagement content.
- Missing media/translation rate.
- Duplicate content rate.
- Content quality review backlog age.
- Remediation link coverage.
- Life-context source/provenance coverage.
- High-risk context disclaimer coverage.

## Ethical growth metrics

- Referral activation rate.
- Share-to-signup conversion.
- Battle rematch rate.
- Battle abandon rate.
- Leaderboard opt-out rate.
- Share privacy opt-out rate.
- Notification unsubscribe rate.
- Reported discomfort or abuse rate.

## Life in Japan learning metrics

- Life-context lesson completion.
- Practical vocabulary added to flashcards.
- Remediation completion after life-context quiz mistakes.
- Useful/confusing feedback by context.
- Sensitive-topic hide/mute rate.
- Finance/gambling ethics gate failure rate.
- Return rate from practical-life content to core BJT study.

## Rules

- Metrics must be backed by real events/rollups.
- Do not use fake charts in production.
- Avoid vanity metrics that do not connect to learning or product health.
- Segment sensitive analytics only when privacy/RBAC rules allow it.
