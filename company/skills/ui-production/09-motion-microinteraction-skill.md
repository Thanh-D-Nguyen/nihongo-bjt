# Motion and Microinteraction Skill

## When to Use

Use for transitions, hover/focus states, loading motion, study feedback, postcards, media, celebrations, battles, and progress moments.

## Required Checks

- Motion has a purpose: orientation, feedback, or progress.
- Reduced-motion users get a safe alternative.
- Timed exam/study flows stay distraction-free.
- Celebrations do not shame or manipulate.
- Audio/video/motion is user-controlled when it can distract.

## Anti-Patterns

- Autoplay media in study/exam flows.
- Looping decorative motion near Japanese reading text.
- Streak pressure or manipulative urgency.
- Motion that hides loading/error state.
- Paywall pressure animations.

## Output Checklist

- motion purpose named
- reduced-motion behavior named
- distraction risk checked
- learner wellbeing risk checked
