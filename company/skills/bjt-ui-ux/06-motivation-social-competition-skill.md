# Motivation, Social, and Competition Skill

## When to Use

Use for streaks, XP, badges, leaderboards, battle, referrals, sharing, postcards, challenges, and comeback loops.

## Required Checks

- Motivation reinforces study effort and mastery.
- Competition is opt-in and privacy-aware.
- Weaker learners are not shamed.
- Leaderboards have privacy mode.
- Battle results link to learning remediation.
- Share cards disclose only user-approved data.
- Referral and growth UI avoids spam pressure.

## Good Patterns

- Personal best over global rank.
- Weekly consistency without streak punishment.
- Battle rematch with practice recommendation.
- Share postcard with editable display name and privacy preview.
- Recovery goals after missed days.

## Anti-Patterns

- Addiction loops or urgency pressure.
- Pay-to-win ranking.
- Publicly exposing weak scores.
- Dark-pattern referral prompts.
- Social prompts before the learner completes core study.

## Output Checklist

- learning benefit named
- privacy mode checked
- shame/dark-pattern risk checked
- remediation link named
