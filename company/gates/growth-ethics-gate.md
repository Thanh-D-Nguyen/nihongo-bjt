# Growth Ethics Gate

Use for referral, sharing, postcards, achievements, battle, leaderboards, streaks, and notifications.

## Pass checklist

- Referral does not spam contacts.
- Sharing is opt-in.
- Public share page and OG metadata are privacy-safe.
- Leaderboard has privacy/identity control where applicable.
- Battle does not shame weaker users.
- Gamification encourages learning, not addiction.
- Competition is fair and anti-cheat-aware.
- Share postcard does not expose email, real name, or private history unless user explicitly chooses.
- Monetized growth is not pay-to-win.
- Notifications respect consent and quiet/focus periods.
- No dark pattern urgency, scarcity, or guilt.
- Finance, lottery, housing, tax, or crypto topics are not used as clickbait growth hooks.

## Evidence

- public page/OG metadata path
- privacy setting path
- battle/leaderboard logic path
- analytics event path
- known gaps and owner

## Blockers

- private data leak
- fake ranks/opponents/share stats
- pay-to-win competition
- spammy referral behavior
- shame-based social comparison
